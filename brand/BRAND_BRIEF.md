## 1. Elevator pitch (paste to describe the app)

**Devoreur** is a real-money, live multiplayer 3D maze game for the Democratic
Republic of Congo. Everyone shares one pitch-black labyrinth. Somewhere inside it
beats the **Heart of the Maze** — the first player to reach it wins the whole
**pot, guaranteed at least 80,000 CDF**. But the dark is not empty: **Dévoreurs de
Lumière** (Light Eaters) — hooded phantoms with glowing green eyes — hunt you by
sound and sight, and a new one joins every 5 minutes. Buy **fireballs** and burn
your rivals to knock them off their path and earn money. It's real francs
congolais: **deposit and withdraw by mobile money**, pay **1,000 CDF** to enter
(4 lives), and join any time. Neon-noir, high-stakes, mobile-first, in French.

## 2. Full description

- **Name:** Devoreur (French, "the Devourer"). Domain: devoreur.com
- **Genre:** Real-money competitive multiplayer, first-person 3D maze / survival hunt
- **Language:** French · **Market:** DR Congo · **Currency:** CDF (Congolese franc), via mobile money (Vodacom M-Pesa, Orange Money, Airtel Money, Africell)
- **Audience:** 18+, mobile gamers, thrill-seekers, mobile-money users across the DRC
- **Core loop:**
  1. Pay a flat **1,000 CDF** to enter → get **4 lives** (join at any time, price never rises).
  2. Explore a huge, dark first-person maze looking for the glowing **Cœur du labyrinthe** (treasure).
  3. Evade the **Dévoreurs de Lumière** — they escalate (a new one every 5 min) and each capture costs a life.
  4. Buy and throw **boules de feu** (fireballs) at rivals — burn a life off them, and the kill pays you.
  5. Reach the Heart first → win the pot (**≥ 80,000 CDF**, house tops it up). Out of lives → pay again or spectate.
  6. **1-hour sessions**; if nobody wins, the pot **rolls over** and keeps growing. Withdraw winnings to mobile money.
- **Hooks:** Real money (not fake) · guaranteed 80,000+ prize · live players across the DRC · easy mobile-money cash-in/out.

## 3. The logo / mascot — "The Eater" (Le Dévoreur)

A **hooded dark phantom**: a smooth ghost/grim-reaper silhouette with a **tattered,
zig-zag cloak hem** at the bottom, no visible mouth or body — just the hood shape
and a dark cavity — with **two round, glowing lime-green eyes** (pale near-white
core) floating in the void. Faint purple "mystic dust" specks around it. It sits on
a **near-black purple-tinted disc / background**. The creature reads as *the thing
that eats the light* — calm, ominous, watching.

- **Wordmark:** `DEVOREUR` in a **bold geometric sans-serif**, wide letter-spacing,
  filled with a **left-to-right gradient lime → cyan → purple**.
- **Layouts that exist:** icon-only (favicon), horizontal (icon + wordmark),
  stacked title lockup (Eater above wordmark + taglines).
