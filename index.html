How to play

Reach the Heart. Win the pot.

Everyone shares one pitch-black maze. Somewhere in it beats the Heart of the Maze. Be the first to reach it and you take the whole pot.

◈ The goal

Find the Heart and touch it before anyone else. The winner claims the pot, and every session is guaranteed to pay at least 15,000 CDF — even if the pot is smaller, the house tops it up.

▸ Getting around

On a computer: WASD to move, mouse to look, Shift to sprint, E to drop a beacon. On a phone: drag to look and use the on-screen stick. Careful — sprinting and beacons make you loud, and the hunters listen.

♥ Your lives

Entry is a flat 1,000 CDF and gives you 4 lives (shown at the top of the screen). You can join at any time — the price never rises. Every time you're caught you lose one life and respawn somewhere new. Run out and you're out of the hunt: pay 1,000 for 4 more lives, or stay and watch.

👁 The hunters

Darkness Eaters prowl the maze, and a new one joins every 5 minutes. They track you by sound and sight — hug the walls, keep moving, and don't get cornered. Each time one catches you, it costs a life.

🔥 Fireballs

Buy fireballs in your wallet, then throw them at rivals. A hit burns one of their lives and knocks them off their path — and getting the kill pays you in Earnings. Use them to stop whoever's closing in on the Heart.

🏆 Winning & the pot

The pot grows from every death and from unused lives when a session ends. Reach the Heart and it's yours (never less than 15,000). If nobody wins within the 1-hour session, everyone is sent home and the pot carries over to the next one — so it just keeps building.

💰 Real money

Deposit by mobile money into your Credit to enter and buy fireballs. Winnings arrive as Earnings, which you can move to Credit to keep playing or cash out to mobile money any time from the wallet.
